# Swiss Ephemeris license gate

This service uses `@swisseph/node`, whose published package is AGPL-3.0. Do not deploy it for Whole Body Earth until the project has deliberately chosen one of these compliant paths:

1. Release the corresponding service source under the AGPL-3.0; or
2. Obtain and document the applicable Swiss Ephemeris Professional license for this service and its client application.

The second path is the expected commercial deployment route. This repository does not include a license grant or proof of purchase.
